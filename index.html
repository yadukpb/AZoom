How We Works ?

Learn about the simple process of renting an electric car with AZoom.

1. Reserve Your Car

Use our online booking system to reserve your electric car. Payment is required via credit card.

2. Pick Up the Car

Visit our office to pick up your reserved car. We have two convenient branches in town.

3. Return the Car

Return the car at a designated car park. Our team will inspect the car for any damages.

4. Final Payment

Pay the final bill including the rental cost and any damages found during the inspection.
